Flavours

1. Tarzan Tikka

A thrilling jungle of flavor! Tender, marinated chicken pieces tossed in a rich, smoky tikka masala sauce, layered over a vibrant garlic base. Topped with fresh cilantro and a cooling drizzle of mint yogurt.

  • Base: Vibrant Garlic Base
  • Protein: Tender, Marinated Chicken Pieces
  • Sauce: Rich, Smoky Tikka Masala Sauce
  • Toppings:
    • Fresh Cilantro
    • Cooling Drizzle of Mint Yogurt

2. Dancing Fajita

Get ready to move! Seasoned strips of beef (or chicken) and bell peppers—sautéed with zesty lime, cumin, and chili—on a bed of mild salsa and melted Monterey Jack cheese.

  • Base: Mild Salsa
  • Cheese: Melted Monterey Jack Cheese
  • Protein Options:
    • Seasoned Strips of Beef
    • Seasoned Strips of Chicken
  • Vegetables & Seasoning:
    • Bell Peppers (Sauteed)
    • Zesty Lime
    • Cumin
    • Chili

3. Wicked Blend

The ultimate indulgence. This pizza features four meticulously layered cheeses (sharp cheddar, creamy Brie, Gorgonzola, and mozzarella) paired with sweet caramelized onions and a subtle touch of truffle oil.

  • Cheeses (Meticulously Layered):
    • Sharp Cheddar
    • Creamy Brie
    • Gorgonzola
    • Mozzarella
  • Aromatics: Sweet Caramelized Onions
  • Finishing Touch: Subtle Touch of Truffle Oil

4. Phantom

A mysteriously light and refreshing white pizza. A base of olive oil, fresh garlic, and ricotta, topped with thinly sliced potatoes, rosemary, and finished with a scattering of sea salt. Perfect for cheese and herb purists.

  • Base: Olive Oil, Fresh Garlic, and Ricotta
  • Toppings:
    • Thinly Sliced Potatoes
    • Rosemary
    • Sea Salt (Finished with a Scattering)
